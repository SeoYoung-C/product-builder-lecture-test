Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information is processed when using Dinner Pick.

1. Information processed

  • This site does not require account signup and does not store directly identifiable personal data on its own server.
  • Theme preference (light/dark) may be saved in your browser local storage for usability.
  • If analytics or ad services are enabled, non-identifiable usage data may be processed under those providers' policies.

2. Purpose of use

  • Preserve UI preferences and improve user experience
  • Understand traffic patterns to improve content quality
  • Support ad delivery and invalid-traffic protection when ads are enabled

3. Third-party processing

The operator does not sell user data. However, third-party providers (analytics/ads) may process technical data according to their own terms.

4. Cookies and controls

You can block or delete cookies in browser settings. Some personalized site features may be limited when cookies are disabled.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, please use the Contact page.

6. Policy changes

Policy updates are reflected on this page with a revised last-updated date.
